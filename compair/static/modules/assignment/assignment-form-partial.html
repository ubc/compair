Schedule

Students can submit and edit their answers only during the answering dates and times you set below. Students do not see an assignment in the application until the answer period begins. Students can read all answers only after the answer period ends and they have completed the required number of comparisons (or any manually set comparison period ends).

By default, students can compare answers after the answer period ends, and self-evaluate after comparing. You may instead set specific dates and times for the comparison and self-evaluation periods.
